You might recognise this

  • You’ve thought about the move properly, but still haven’t made it.
  • You keep circling the same next step without starting.
  • You’re not short of ideas. You’re short of movement.
  • You can see other people progressing and feel like you should be further on.
  • Something is off, but you can’t quite work out what the real issue is.

This usually isn’t a lack of effort.

Most people assume they need more confidence, more discipline, or a better plan. But when you already know the move and still are not making it, the problem is usually somewhere else.

Something is getting in the way of action.

The GAP Profile is designed to help you pinpoint what that is.

What it looks at

  • How much you trust your own capability
  • How risky the next step feels
  • Whether the move feels like you
  • Whether you actually have the time, energy, and headspace to act
